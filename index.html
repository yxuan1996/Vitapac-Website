About Us

We are Malaysia's leading repacker of Desiccant/Silica Gel, and we are an ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified company. We repack Desiccant and Silica Gel that are effective in adsorbing moisture and reducing humidity in a sealed environment.

Our counterparts in China produce high quality silica gel according to the specifications endorsed by the Japanese, European & American authorities.

Our factory includes temperature and humidity controlled rooms, 60 units of packing machines, automatic loader and heater, allowing us to pack to our customer's required specifications. Our products undergo extensive quality control, allowing us to deliver high quality at competitive prices.

We have our in-house laboratory with stand - up oven, humidity chamber and moisture analyzer equipment for incoming and outgoing testing on all desiccant/silica gel .
